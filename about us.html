ABOUT US

Almajiri Child Watch Foundation is a non- profit and non- political organization established on March of 2018 by a group of young individuals, with the sole aim of catering for the needs of the Almajiri child in order to make a change in a society where these children are being neglected on
the streets and subjected to child labour, begging or even worst.

“Almajiri” comes from the arabic word “Al-Muhajirun” which means “the emigrants”; it is
a common term used for kids who migrate to seek Islamic knowledge. Almajiri also means student.
